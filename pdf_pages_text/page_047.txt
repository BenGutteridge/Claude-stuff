24. The Unfair Subway

Marvin gets off work at random times between 3 and 5 p.m. His
mother lives uptown, his girl friend downtown. He takes the
first subway that comes in either direction and eats dinner with the
one he is first delivered to. His mother complains that he never
comes to see her, but he says she has a 50-50 chance. He has had
dinner with her twice in the last 20 working days. Explain.

Solution for The Unfair Subway

Downtown trains run past Marvin's stop at, say, 3:00, 3:10, 3:20, ... ,etc.,
and uptown trains at 3:01, 3:11, 3:21,.... To go uptown Marvin must
arrive in the 1-minute interval between a downtown and an uptown train.
